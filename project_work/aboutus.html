Introducing Our Dishes

BIRYANI


Biryani is prepared in an earthen pot called Handi, the rice and meat are layered, bottom and top layer are always rice. An interlayer of some condiments is introduced between the meat and the rice.

Hyderabadi Biryani is the kachay gosht ki Biryani or the dum Biryani, where the meat is marinated and cooked along with the rice.

Lucknowni or Malabar Biryani is a pakki Biryani. The rice and meat are generally partially cooked separately in spiced water, then layered and cooked by the dum pukht method.

It is a sub variant of Lucknowi style of Biryani.SS uses one of the most expensive branded rice, stringently selected meat pieces and vegetables, Kerala carefully sourced spices, authentic age old recipes to make its Biryani.

KURMA


Korma is a noble dish that once graced the banquet tables of the imperial Mughal court, rich with cream and nuts and heavily scented with expensive spice, has become one of the most loved dishes in the world.

At SS, The Korma is made by marinating the main ingredients in yoghurt, cashew paste and fresh ground spices to produce a mouth-watering thick sauce or glaze. We believe that SS serves the best mutton, chicken & veg kormas in the country.

GRILL CHICKEN


Grilled chicken is so versatile that most cultures have some variation of the dish: Mexico's pollo asado, Indonesia's chicken satay, Caribbean jerk chicken, American barbecue — the list goes on and on.

These dishes each have distinct flavors, but they all start with perfectly cooked poultry: moist, tender, and juicy with a lovely golden brown crispy skin kissed by a few lines of grill marks. 

PHIRNI


Originated during the Mughal Empire in India, BBK’s Special Phirni made with Traditional method will surely bring water in your mouth.

They are cooked on slow fire, simmered for hours till they turn creamy and then flavoured, garnished with chopped pistachios, almonds and saffron.

The most unique thing about BBK Phirni apart from taste is the earthen clay pot in which it is served making the experience most authentic. Not to be missed with ANY BBK ORDER!
